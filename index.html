Vehicle Prediction Models

MPG Regression






















Typical Value Ranges:
Cylinders: 3 to 8
Displacement: 60 to 460 cubic inches
Horsepower: 40 to 240
Weight: 1500 to 5500 pounds
Acceleration: 8 to 26 seconds
Model Year: 70 to 82
Origin: 1=USA, 2=Europe, 3=Asia

These ranges roughly match the distribution of the Auto MPG dataset the model was trained on.

Cylinder Classifier






















Common Cylinder Categories:
4 cylinders: Smaller, more efficient cars
6 cylinders: Mid-size vehicles and light trucks
8 cylinders: Larger trucks and muscle cars

This model assigns a probability to each cylinder class and returns the most likely one.
